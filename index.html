Welcome!

In this game, your goal is to match three or more items of the same kind,
either in row or column. To do it, select an item and then the adjacent item to swap places.

If you create a group of items, those items will be removed and you will earn points.
Keep making groups of three elements or more before the time runs out!

Controls

Left click: select

Enter or space: select

Arrows or WASD: move and swap

New game

Select a difficulty level
